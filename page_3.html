Banana Bread Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 overripe bananas
  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
    •

    Instructions

    Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

    Mash bananas in a bowl.

    Add flour, baking soda, egg and butter. Mix.

    Optionally, for a sweeter bread, add 1/2 cup sugar and vanilla extract. Mix.

    Bake for 20 minutes in a 4x8 inch oven-safe loaf pan. Enjoy!
